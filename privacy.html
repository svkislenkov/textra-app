Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Textra is a student project created at the University of Michigan for educational purposes. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect

We only collect the phone number you voluntarily provide when opting in to receive reminder or notification messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to send reminder or notification messages that you request within the Textra app. We do not sell, share, or use your information for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at skis@umich.edu. All data will be permanently deleted upon request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect project changes or feedback. The most current version will always be available on this page.
